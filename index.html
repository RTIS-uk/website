About RTIS

Grounded in Retail. Built for Delivery.

RTIS was established by retail operations professionals with extensive hands-on experience delivering large-scale technology installations and merchandising programmes for major UK grocery and retail chains. We understand what it takes to execute in live retail environments — because we have done it, at scale, to the highest standards.

  • Proven track record delivering ESL rollouts across major UK supermarket estates, completed within agreed overnight and weekend windows without a single instance of live trading disruption
  • Experienced in coordinating large multi-disciplinary installation teams across multiple concurrent sites, maintaining consistent quality and compliance throughout
  • Deep working knowledge of retail compliance requirements, go-live sign-off processes, and Environmental Health Officer documentation standards
  • Merchandising expertise spanning planogram implementation, full range resets, new store setups, and seasonal remodels — delivered with precision and speed
  • A management team with commercial qualifications and operational expertise, bringing strategic clarity to every project we undertake

How We Work

A Structured Approach to Every Project

Our proven methodology protects your timeline, your brand, and your customers at every stage of delivery.

01

Scoping & Planning

We review your full project requirements — site details, SLA windows, compliance obligations, and resource needs — before committing a single team member to site.

02

Team Allocation

The right technicians and merchandisers are matched to each project based on skills, location, and experience. Schedules are confirmed in advance with no last-minute changes.

03

Deployment & Execution

Our teams execute within your agreed windows — overnight, weekend, or daytime. Real-time progress updates keep all stakeholders informed throughout the installation.

04

Sign-Off & Reporting

Digital compliance sign-off, audit-ready project records, and a full completion report are delivered on conclusion of every engagement. No chasing. No gaps.
